Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Durban, South Africa, GreenEarth Community Garden is a non-profit initiative dedicated to promoting urban agriculture, environmental sustainability, and healthy living through community collaboration.

🌍 Mission

To empower communities through sustainable gardening, environmental education, and hands-on training for self-sufficiency.

🌱 Vision

A future where every household has access to fresh, healthy, and affordable food grown in harmony with nature.
